荣誉与奖项Honors & Awards

院长嘉奖名单：杰出学术成就 Dean's List: Outstanding Academic Achievement

亚利桑那州立大学 (Arizona State University) Arizona State University

院长嘉奖名单授予那些成功修满至少十二个学分且学期平均绩点达到3.5或以上的学生。我转学到亚利桑那州立大学后的第一个学期就获得了这项荣誉，之后几个学期也都获得了该奖项。 Dean's List recognition is granted to students who successfully completed a minimum of twelve credit hours and achieved a semester grade point average of 3.5 or higher. I first received this award in my first semester after transferring to Arizona State University, and again in subsequent semesters.
